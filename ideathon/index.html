ABOUT IDEATHON

Ideathon is all about showcasing ideas and weighing their potential benefits to society. It is an ideal opportunity for you to provide a viable solution to society's problems or to come up with the best possible cocktail of idea and innovation. NMIT's ideathon is tailored to provide participants a first hand trial to the Entrepreneurial journey. It offers a competitive model for showcasing original thinking and problem solving aptitiude among the participants.

OUR VISION

We at ECELL - NMIT believe that the only constant is change. We want to be a part of the transformation that will shape new businesses. As a result, with ideathon we aim to provide an opportunity for unique ideas, fresh approaches, and new solutions. We want our participants to rethink, redefine, and broaden the concept of entrepreneurship.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the last date of submission?

The last date for registration is 30th April,2022.

Can I submit additional documents/ppts along with the description asked?

No, we do not accept any other documents. The judging will be purely based on the asked questionnaire.

Can a team consist of people of different backgrounds or colleges?

Yes, teams can consist of individuals from different backgrounds or colleges/companies.

What is the registration/participation fee for Ideathon 3.0?

There is no registration fee for round1 as it is an Online Round. As ECELL is a non-profit organization working for development of gen-z and budding entrepreneurs, for Round2 a nominal fee of Rs 100/- will be charged from the teams selected for the final offline round.
